Project Ideas

Overcome

Nowadays, people are very concerned about health as well as eating and drinking during the day. My project idea is to create an application that helps the user to choose the food for each meal of the day. The app will be used by the user’s own smartphone, while the user can easily download and use it. The main meals of the day as well as snacks this app will suggest what to eat at this time. In addition, how much water is needed each day for the body, this app will announce the best time frames to replenish the body.

Motivation

Actually, I am a person who cares about health. Recently, I am especially interested in nutrition because I want to have a balanced body for better health. So, I really want to create an application about meal service to improve my nutrition as well as everyone else. According to the National Institute of Nutrition of Vietnam, in the daily meals of families, there is always a 30-50% deficiency in mineral content. essential substances for the body. Therefore, using this meal support application, in families, especially housewives will no longer have a headache for family meals and young people will provide full energy for youth.

Description

When users use this application, they will need to fill in necessary information such as weight, height, age, and describe their own health condition such as illness and daily nutrition. The application will ask a few questions to correctly determine your body condition such as whether you are in a state of lack of sleep, stress, or often using stimulant drinks such as alcohol or coffee. After determining the user’s essential information, the app will start making a list of which foods to eat. Each timeframe will have a food list consisting of a meal and snack; and time frames for best results when drinking water. For example, 6 am-7am, drinking water after a long sleep will help cleanse the liver and kidneys. The main breakfast menu will be a sandwich with a glass of milk, so the snack will be a banana like that was enough energy for one morning. Every day the application will ask questions about your health status today, if that situation you have problems such as induction, the application will change the menu for that day to switch to your suitable dishes. state. If you are the one who takes care of a family meal consisting of many members, the application will receive information about each member of your family and set up a menu suitable for the whole family. Nowadays housewives have a headache in thinking about implementing the application suitable for the whole family. When using this application, the dishes will have simple suggestions for processing such as how many tablespoons sugar content and especially the application does not recommend using fried food. Therefore, nutrition of family members will improve and especially for children in the developing period, they will be developed in the best way. To gets enough vitamins for the body, each day the application will suggest a different juice drink and each time it will be a different vitamin. With the meal setting features suitable for everyone, it will help people have good health as well as have energy to work. The application will be left to default on the notification frame of the phone, you just scroll down to see what time to add to the body and especially it will not notify continuously because you just need to drag the default bar. Down is you can see what you need. Those are the feature details of the meal assistant.

Tool and Technologies

I use the MIT and Android mobile app invention tool for smartphone app development. I use the MIT App inventor to code my project because the tool uses a Java software called App Inventor Extra which is in charge of controlling the Android phone. After writing the application code, I will connect to the android for the software to run. Finally, I will put it on google play for everyone to use. Since the MIT App inventor tool applies only to the Android operating system and Android user only can use this app.

Skill required

The skill required to create my project is first of all to have the creative idea to create the best project for the user, being up to date on culinary innovations. Data analysis skills are essential in retrieving and distributing user information. Nutrition skills, as this is a human health project, it is necessary for those who specialize in nutrition to analyze the amount of food needed by the user. For software skills, python and SQL writing skills are required for data storage. If there is a test to find the necessary skills, software and hardware, I will analyze and choose which one is really suitable and necessary for my project.

Outcome

If the project is successful, a user meal support application will be created. With this project, all users will have a good health proper meal. People can use it offline when not online and easily manipulate when using. If a problem occurs in the first place, I check what the problem is and fix it immediately. Developing a nutrition application on the phone is very convenient because nowadays almost everyone owns a phone and the pace of the world is quite fast, so everyone becomes more and more busy at work. do not care about their own nutrition. Therefore, the development of this application helps people to have full nutrition and not underestimate their health.
